Ingredient Endeavors is a meal planning application used to help problem solve weekly and day to day meal planning challenges based on ingredients on hand.

Meal planning has many challenges including time, limited ingredients, budgets, and dietary restrictions. The goal of I.E. is to ease these stresses by providing options through a search of ingredients to make meal planning easy and approachable.
